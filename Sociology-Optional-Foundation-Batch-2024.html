  • There are 4 Tests included in this Sociology Foundation batch where a general understanding is given that how to approach an answer in analytical way. NOW WHY FEW TESTS ONLY WITH FOUNDATION BATCH AND NOT DAILY ANSWER WRITING? Daily Answer Writing is a MYTH. There are obvious reasons that there is inter-linkage of topics in Sociology, one topic is by and large connected with other topics. Also there is comparative questions where we have to compare one scholars view with another. So unless and until students learn entire sociology, unless they revise it at least 3-4 times and understand and memorize concepts, unless they ‘INTERNALIZE’ Sociology properly they can not write analytical marks fetching answers. Because of these obvious reasons students only attempt 1 or 2 test, they realize the reality and they stop writing further tests. So we have kept 4 tests to give a general but effective understanding of how to approach an answer.
