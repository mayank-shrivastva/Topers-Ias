About Us

Storey Tellers was borne out of a vision to design and visualize spaces that hold a strong narrative to inspire and evoke a sensorial experience for our clientele. We design and build spaces that are intuitive, responsive and ingenious envisioning spaces to be pragmatic with personalized aesthetics of our clients we collaborate with. Our creation process of mindfully working with our clientele makes it an effortless experience.

Our team comprises Interior Designers, Architects and experienced project managers to ensure a smooth and fun process of designing and ensuring seamless execution.

OUR FOUNDERS

TANBEER KAZI

Tanbeer brings in energy, enthusiasm and infectious vibes to the organization. With his down-to-earth and practical approach towards problems, he takes on challenges with a straight forward and simplistic attitude. An architect from NIT Bhopal, he’s gained extensive experience while working at the Novotel group of hotels, Biba in India and Dubai. KOMAL RUSTAGI

Komal is passionate about all things design. To visualize and create spaces that tell a story has been the driving force that drove her to delve deeper into the field of Interior Architecture. After completing her Bachelors in Architecture, she studied design from Domus Academy, Milan. Having worked with eminent architectural firms in Delhi and London, she’s picked up the nuances of design detailing prevalent in modern design.

PRAKASH SRIVASTAVA

OUR SERVICE

Understanding client’s vision and executing those into structures and spaces, we undertake a wide array of services to cater to individual and personalized requirements of our clientele. These services may be taken independently or packaged together to design your project which may be a residence, retail space, office or restaurant.

DESIGN

A conceptual process in collaboration with clients where we translate their vision and integrate the brief given. We take up Interior Design, Architecture and Interior Styling services.

BUILD

We take up turnkey projects until Handover to make sure the vision created is executed well with help of our in-house vendors, quantity surveyors, project managers and Supervisors.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

We take up full project management to ensure smooth and timely execution of the project with minimal cost escalations ensuring complete supervision and estimates.
