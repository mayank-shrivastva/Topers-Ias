OUR SERVICE


Understanding client’s vision and executing those into structures and spaces, we undertake a wide array of services to cater to individual and personalized requirements of our clientele. These services may be taken independently or packaged together to design your project which may be a residence, retail space, office or restaurant.


DESIGN

A conceptual process in collaboration with clients where we translate their vision and integrate the brief given. We take up Interior Design, Architecture and Interior Styling services.

BUILD

We take up turnkey projects until Handover to make sure the vision created is executed well with help of our in-house vendors, quantity surveyors, project managers and Supervisors.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

We take up full project management to ensure smooth and timely execution of the project with minimal cost escalations ensuring complete supervision and estimates.
